About me

Hi, I'm Cezary, a CompSci student with a passion for game development, software engineering and algorithmics. I've lived in London for 8 years from 2010 till 2018, so saying I'm comfortable with my English skills would be quite an understatement. During my days in secondary school I developed an interest in game development, which I nurtured by studying the Unity game engine.

Alongside my Unity journey, I also studied C++ and algorithmics, which I too find myself very comfortable with, having even taught High school students in the past academic year. And although I live and breath computing, I do enjoy playing guitar, drawing and sketching, exercising, listening to some good music and playing games (mostly RPGs and casual action games). I also accept graphic design commisions for a variety of projects.
